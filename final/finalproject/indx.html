My Culture

I am a first-generation American, but my parents are both from a country in East Africa called Eritrea. In Eritrean culture, you will often see colorful zuriyas (traditional dresses), coffee - also known as boon - and itan (scented cubes). These items are often used during family gatherings and events. They remind me of my childhood and represent an aspect of my identity.
